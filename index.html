About Sundrops Day Spa

Sundrops Day Spa offers an emerging fresh and original concept to the usual hand and foot care services. Located in the heart of SM Megamall, we provide a serene escape from the bustling city life.

Our skilled technicians are dedicated to delivering exceptional nail care and spa treatments in a relaxing environment. Whether you need a quick manicure or a full pampering session, we're here to help you look and feel your best.
